Why per-truck phones (not per-person)

Each crew truck (T-007 = Crew A, T-009 = Crew B, etc.) has a dedicated mounted phone. The phone stays with the truck. Whoever's the foreman that day signs in. Field crews rotate, foremen swap, but the phone-to-truck mapping stays stable. Saves the cost of issuing per-person devices and ensures GPS + WEX vehicle data lines up cleanly with the right truck.

Step 1 — Language picker

First screen on app open: English or Español. Setting is sticky to the phone but can be changed anytime in Settings. Most LOVING crew speak Spanish at home, English at work, or both. Letting them pick reduces friction and errors when reading WO scope or Sarah Mitchell's CM notes.

App prompts: "Sign in to Truck T-007." Foreman dropdown defaults to J. Reyes (Crew A · usual). Other foremen are listed as backup options for cases where someone's filling in. PIN is 4 digits, set up by Jim Howie when phone was provisioned.
